About the RSSS

An archipelago located in the southern part of the Indian Ocean

The country, which was founded in 1983 after the great Sherkanese Revolution of May, has lived through the fall of the USSR and now is a privileged partner of the People's Republics of China and North Korea and the only country in the world to recognise officialy the existence of the Sovietic Republic of Transnistria.
